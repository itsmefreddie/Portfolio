The
Age of Airplane Trailer

As I said, this is the first video edit I made as I was learning video editing through DaVinci Resolve Training webiste. I made this edit as close as possible to the instructor's guidance.
I cut and added a bunch of different clips together, colour corrected, added transitions and fusion titles. I also added audio transitions, sound effects (like the plane taking off), and a background music.
As a first time edit, it took me hours to make this short clip, and I hope you enjoy it!
